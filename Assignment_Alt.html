Elephants

Elephants are very unique creatures. Here you will learn some fascinating facts about those gentle giants.

Physical attributes

Elephants have many distinguishing physical traits. They can be characterized by their trunks, tusks, and large stature.

Trunk

An elephants trunk has many uses and is one of the most distinguishing traits of an elephant.
